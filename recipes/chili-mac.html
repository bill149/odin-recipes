Fire Chili Mac

This is a recipe that's easy to make and tastes FIRE!

So lets get started

Ingredients:

  • Bag of macaroni
  • Two cans chili beans
  • Half pound of ground burger
  • Medium onion
  • Bottle of the hottest picante sauce you can find
  • Bag of mexican three-cheese blend
  • Couple spoonfuls of olive oil

Now it's time to put this thing together

Instructions:

  1. Chop your onion and cook until soft in olive oil, then add your burger and brown
  2. Meanwhile, you'll be boiling your noodles
  3. Drain your burger and onions, add your picante sauce and cooked noodles and let simmer
  4. When done to your taste serve topped with a healthy topping of your three-cheese blend

    5. Hope you enjoy

    Note: I don't use salt and pepper; the picante sauce provides all the seasoning I need. But feel free to use as much salt as your system can handle
