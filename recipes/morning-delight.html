Morning Delight

A Delightful Morning Sugar Fix

It's Saturday morning and you're pumped for a weekend shot of pure corn syrup. Well this bad mammy jammy is the perfect remedy!

Lets get started:

  • A box of your sweetest, most sugar-saturated cereal. I like Fruity Pebbles
  • A half gallon of your favorite ice cream. I'm partial to vanilla, though any will work
  • Some chunks of chocolate, or a snack cake or two, or even some chopped oreos
  • Eight or more ounces of whole milk, or skim if you prefer.

Now comes the good part

  1. First, fill a large bowl with your cereal
  2. Then layer your toppings on top of your cereal
  3. Next, smother your bowl of goodies with milk
  4. And lastly top your bowl of goodness with a couple scoops (large ones, mind you) of your favorite ice cream.

I hope you enjoy (and survive) this massive dose of morning sugar. And please don't try this if you're diabetic!
